Amsterdam

What an amazing trip this was! Amsterdam is one of the most amazing places in the entire world! Jared and I spent two weeks in the incredible city. We saw incredible works of art, rode roller coasters, experienced a very confusing transit system, enjoyed some of the freshest produce I have ever had, boated down canals, smoked pot in public, tripped on truffels, and visited an enchanting zoo.

This is a place I will never forget. Since I was a teenager I always dreamed of visiting Amsterdam. A place of peace and fun where you could enjoy a joint in a public park or a coffee shop. I always wanted to visit the shop, "Azarius", where they sold truffles of all kinds. I made this dream come true!
