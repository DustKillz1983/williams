Hobbies.....

Another one of my passions is collecting things. I love to collect all types of cards, from basketball to Pokemon. Basketball cards are probably my most favorite item to collect. I know almost all of the NBA players right now and through running the card shop I am learning much more. I still don't know how to play football, but I am starting to grasp all of the player's names. I have a nice collection of basketball, football, and baseball cards and I am working on other sports like soccer. I also sell a lot of Pokemon cards at the shop so I am trying to learn all I can about those as well. I will post my favorite cards and other collectibles on here for all to see!
