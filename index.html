Welcome to Dusty's Site! I am currently taking some web design courses through Coursera's online learning program. So far I am really enjoying it. This site is a result of what I am learning through them. At the beginning I only knew some basic HTML, but now I have graduated on to CSS which I would like to think I am fairly proficient with thus far. My next course is going to be exploring Javascript which I am excited about. I know nothing about it at the moment but as I learn over the duration of the course I will be applying it in real time to this site!

Now that you know why I am creating this site, let me tell you what it is about. The site is all about me. It is going to be a type of journal which is going to include my interests, my work, where I live, and anything else that I feel like sharing. I am going to include a section on my vacations, my hobbies, and things I am excited about.

As I learn more about web design I will be incorporating it here. Hopefully, I will be adding elements like images, audio, video, forms, navigation, and animations. I am not really sure who my audience for this site will be, if anyone at all besides me, but I am excited to see it develop.

I've been to Gatlinburg, TN, Biltmore Mansion in Asheville, NC, Amsterdam, and Florida. I have also been to London and Paris but that was before the age of digital cameras and iPhones so I will have to scan some of those pictures in later.
